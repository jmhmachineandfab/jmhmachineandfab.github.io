OUR SERVICES

MACHINING

We have a fully equipped machining workshop for milling, boring, drilling, and turning up to 19" diameter and 60" length, specializing in hydraulic cylinder repair.

FABRICATION

We have the ability to cut and process 4' x 8' sheets up to 3/4", with a press brake forming up to 11ga and 33" of bend length.

WELDING

We offer a wide range of welding capabilities, including Stick, MIG, and TIG, with the ability to weld stainless, mild steel, cast iron, and aluminum.

ABOUT

JMH Machine & Fabrication is a locally owned and operated shop in South Central Texas. We are dedicated to helping our neighbors and communities across the state keep their operations running smooth.

Whether you are a local farmer needing an implement modification, in the oilfield looking for a precision part, or a business owner with a custom project in mind, we've got you covered. We handle everything from manual machining and experienced welding to heavy-duty repairs and custom fabrication.

We understand that when your equipment is down, it's costing you money. We focus on fast turnaround times without sacrificing quality. With a dedication to superior craftsmanship and exceptional customer service, we turn raw metal into reliable solutions for agricultural, industrial, and local customers. Let's bring your vision to life.
